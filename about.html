Welcome to Shusha City

The Shusha City Card is your gateway to discovering the rich cultural and historical heritage of Shusha. Designed to enhance the visitor experience, the card provides exclusive access to top attractions, discounts at local businesses, and convenient payment solutions throughout the city. Explore Shusha with ease and immerse yourself in the vibrant history of one of Azerbaijan’s most treasured cities.

Our Mission and Vision

Our mission is to enrich the visitor experience in Shusha by providing easy access to cultural sites, supporting sustainable tourism, and fostering a deeper connection with the city’s heritage. We aim to promote Shusha’s historical significance and make it accessible to everyone while preserving its cultural integrity.

Our vision is to establish Shusha City Card as a model for cultural tourism in Azerbaijan and beyond, bridging the past and the present to inspire future generations. We envision a future where visitors and locals alike can celebrate Shusha’s heritage, contributing to a thriving, sustainable community.

Our Story

The Shusha City Card project was born out of a deep respect for Shusha’s heritage. Known as the "Cradle of Azerbaijani Culture," Shusha holds a unique place in our nation’s history, with ancient landmarks, rich traditions, and a vibrant cultural legacy. Our journey began with a vision to make Shusha more accessible while preserving its historical integrity.

Since our inception, we've achieved key milestones, including partnerships with local businesses, collaborations with cultural organizations, and the development of a digital platform for seamless visitor experiences. The Shusha City Card has grown into an essential tool for exploring and appreciating Shusha’s cultural treasures.

About Shusha Şəhər Dövlət Qoruq İdarəsi

Shusha Şəhər Dövlət Qoruq İdarəsi is dedicated to preserving and restoring the historical, cultural, and architectural heritage of Shusha, one of Azerbaijan’s most treasured cities. The organization plays a key role in maintaining Shusha as a living museum and a symbol of Azerbaijani heritage by restoring and protecting ancient landmarks, including historical buildings, monuments, and cultural sites. It also focuses on cultural education by organizing educational events, exhibitions, and tours to raise awareness about the city’s rich history and significance.

Community Impact

Economic Growth

The Shusha City Card stimulates economic growth by attracting tourists who support local businesses, restaurants, and shops. Increased foot traffic contributes to Shusha’s economic revitalization.

Support for Artisans

We work closely with local artisans, showcasing their handicrafts and cultural products to tourists. This provides sustainable income for artisans and enriches the visitor experience with authentic Azerbaijani culture.

Preservation of Heritage

Revenue from the Shusha City Card supports the preservation of cultural landmarks and historical sites. By promoting sustainable tourism, we help ensure that Shusha’s heritage remains intact for future generations.

Sustainability and Future Goals

Our Commitment to Sustainability

The Shusha City Card is dedicated to promoting sustainable tourism by minimizing environmental impacts. By encouraging eco-friendly practices and supporting local artisans, we aim to create a responsible and enriching experience for visitors. Revenue from the card helps fund projects that protect Shusha’s natural and cultural resources.

Looking Ahead

We envision an evolving Shusha City Card that continually adds value for visitors. Our future goals include expanding digital guides, increasing partnerships with local businesses, and launching eco-tourism initiatives. We aim to make Shusha an accessible, sustainable destination for future generations.
